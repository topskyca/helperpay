FAQ

常見問題

Is it free?
收唔收費？
Completely free during beta. If it becomes an official product there may be a small fee later — beta users will be told well in advance.
Beta 測試期間完全免費。如果日後推出正式版可能會收取少量費用 — 屆時會提早通知測試用戶。
What if my rest-day or holiday arrangement is different?
我同姐姐的休息日／假期安排唔同點算？
Everything is adjustable: the weekly rest day, individual dates (tap any day on the calendar), the holiday list, and the first-week rule. If something doesn't fit, WhatsApp us — that's exactly the feedback we want.
全部可以自訂：每週休息日、個別日子（喺日曆撳一下就改到）、假期清單、首週例假規則。如果有安排配合唔到，請 WhatsApp 我哋 — 呢啲正正係我哋想收集嘅意見。
My helper worked a statutory holiday — can I just pay her extra instead of a day off?
姐姐喺法定假日返咗工，可唔可以只補錢、唔補假？
No — this is the one thing the law flatly forbids, even if she agrees: any payment in lieu of a statutory holiday is an offence (fine HK$50,000). The legal way works for everyone: give 48 hours' notice, pay the extra day voluntarily if you wish (HelperPay adds it to the statement), and schedule an alternative day off within 60 days — the app reminds you and keeps the record. She gets the money and a movable day off. (Rest days are different: rest-day work must be voluntary, but compensating it with extra pay is lawful by mutual agreement — HelperPay calculates that too.)
唔可以 — 就算姐姐同意都唔得：以任何金錢代替法定假日屬違法（罰款 $50,000）。合法做法其實雙方都受惠：預早 48 小時通知，額外一日人工可以自願照付（HelperPay 會計入糧單），再喺 60 日內安排一日補假 — App 會提醒你並保留紀錄。姐姐有錢收，仲有一日可以另揀日子嘅補假。（休息日就唔同：休息日返工必須出於自願，但以額外人工作補償係雙方同意就合法 — HelperPay 一樣計到。）
Do statutory holidays apply in the first 3 months?
頭 3 個月有冇法定假日？
The day off applies from day one for every employee. Statutory holiday pay under the Ordinance starts after 3 months — but for monthly-paid helpers the monthly wage covers those days anyway, so in practice nothing is deducted. HelperPay follows this from day one.
假期本身由第一日開始就有，人人一樣。法例上嘅假期薪酬要滿 3 個月先開始計 — 但月薪制外傭嘅月薪本身已包咗呢啲日子，實際上唔會扣人工。HelperPay 由第一日已經照此處理。
Can my helper use her own phone?
工人姐姐可唔可以用自己手機？
For now the household shares one phone (helper mode makes that safe). A version with separate logins and sync between phones is on the roadmap.
現階段一家共用一部手機（外傭模式確保安全）。獨立登入、兩部手機同步的版本已在開發計劃之中。
I changed phones — how do I move my data?
換咗手機，資料點搬？
Old phone: Settings → Export backup (send the file to yourself). New phone: install the app → Settings → Import backup.
舊手機：Settings → Export backup（將檔案傳給自己）。新手機：安裝 App → Settings → Import backup。
Is the calculation really correct?
計算真係準確？
The salary engine has an automated test suite that reproduces independently verified real payroll cases to the cent, and the rules follow the Labour Department's guides. You can always check the breakdown line by line in the statement.
計糧引擎有自動測試，可以一仙不差地重現經獨立核實的真實糧單，規則依照勞工處指引。糧單每一項都列得清清楚楚，隨時可以逐行核對。
